About Elinara

Engineering reliability for businesses across Africa.

A professional engineering company built on two decades of field experience and a culture of solving the problems other vendors avoid.

Elinara Engineering Ltd is a professional engineering company established to deliver reliable technical solutions for businesses, institutions, government agencies, printing companies, and industrial clients.

With over two decades of hands-on engineering experience, we provide expert installation, maintenance, troubleshooting, refurbishment, and technical consulting services across large-format printing, telecommunications, security and public address systems.

We are led by Adedeji Emmanuel Owoyemi, an engineering professional with over 26 years of practical field experience, supported by a team of specialists who share his commitment to craftsmanship and accountability.
